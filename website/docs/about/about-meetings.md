# Meetings

Join our community meetings to discuss AboutCode projects, ask questions, and collaborate with the team.

## Weekly Community Meeting

*   **When**: Mondays at 15:00 UTC
*   **Topic**: General updates, GSoC, and Q&A.
*   **Where**: https://meet.jit.si/AboutCode

## Weekly VulnerableCode Meeting

*   **When**: Tuesdays at 15:00 UTC
*   **Topic**: Discussion of the [VulnerableCode project](https://github.com/aboutcode-org/vulnerablecode)
*   **Where**: https://meet.jit.si/AboutCode

## Bi-weekly PURL Community Meeting
*   **When**: Wednesdays at 17:00 UTC - every two weeks starting January 7
*   **Topic**: Discussion of the [PURL](https://github.com/package-url/purl-spec)
and [VERS](https://github.com/package-url/vers-spec) specifications and 
related projects
*   **Where**: https://meet.google.com/ryq-aimn-ghd 

## Join the Conversation

You can also join our community via chat:
*   [Gitter / Element](https://app.gitter.im/#/room/#aboutcode-org_discuss:gitter.im)
There are Gitter channels for the major AboutCode projects and for GSoC.
*   [Slack](https://join.slack.com/t/aboutcode-org/shared_invite/zt-1paqwxccw-IuafuiAvYJFkTqGaZsC1og)
There are Slack channels for most AboutCode projects and for topics like 
supply chain security. The project channels include notifications from GitHub 
to help you track project activity.
