## Past Events

### FOSDEM 2026

We regularly participate in **FOSDEM**, one of the largest gatherings of Free 
and Open Source Software developers in the world. The 2026 conference was
held on January 31 and February 1 in Brussels, Belgium.

Since 2024 AboutCode has hosted a workshop before FOSDEM to bring together 
users and developers of FOSS tools for license and security compliance. 
The 2026 **FOSS license and security compliance tools workshop** was
held on January 30, 2026 at the Hotel Bedford in Brussels.
